Join us in the after-hours of Somniverse.


The Dream Office: Work and Learn While You Sleep

For students and professionals, Somniverse presents the next evolution of the workplace. With dedicated dream spaces, we have cultivated environments and modules that are optimized for learning and retention, motivation and ideation. You set the level of lucidity- from passive and subliminal to fully aware and active. Some of humanity's greatest inventions awoke from sleeping minds- now your vision can be shared instantly with colleages and creators. The Dream Office transforms rest into opportunity.



The DreamBook

Somniverse began as a collaboration between scientists and artists who were tired of ideas slipping through their fingers before finding a pen or brush to record them. The DreamBook allows you to save dreams as you experience them, return and edit, then relive or share them. Add your own pages to the catalog of dreams, or browse the DreamBook to select and experience the inner worlds of your favorite dreamers. A dedicated staff of professional artists, writers and educators contributes new content daily, ever expanding our Somniverse.


Zero Cost Model: Recycling the Unused Mind

Access to the Somniverse carries no monetary charge*. Instead, participation supports the broader ecosystem by using your dormant neural regions for distributed computation. These processes help power both the Somniverse and global research initiatives, as well as reduce the environmental footprint of traditional data centers. Your nightly presence helps sustain the network, advance collective creativity, and keep Somniverse accessible to all.
